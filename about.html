About the Layer Chicken Project

The Layer Chicken Project was started to promote sustainable poultry farming and ensure a steady supply of fresh eggs for the community. Our focus is on healthy chickens, efficient production, and providing nutritious food.

Project Highlights

  • Started in 2023 with 50 chicks
  • Daily monitoring of egg production
  • Focus on sustainable farming practices
  • Supplying fresh eggs to the local community
